About Us

Rotaract Club Jammu City is an enthusiastic club of the young blood of Jammu. The club operates with the central aim of social welfare while preparing its members to serve for the greater good of the society through every avenue that they pursue.

Tawi cleanathon

Our team with the support of Climate front, Jammu amd F.O.R.T cleaned almost 300 kg of non-biodegradable waste.

Project Suraksha

A vaccination drive was organized on 9th August, 2021 at Urban health centre, Trikuta Nagar, Jammu.

PAWsible together

Focusing on the condition of the stray dogs in chilling winter season we provided them with cozy beds and shelter.

BAKE SALE

A Bake Sale was organized on the eve of Christmas wherein we sold muffins to the general public as a fund raising project to buy woolen clothes for the less fortunate.

Project Annapurna

A food donation drive was organized with the purpose to distribute the unused/leftover food from the weddings to feed less fortunate people around the city.

Blanket & Cloth donation drive

A cloth donation drive was organized on 12th November, 2020 where the volunteers of RCJC distributed clithes among the less fortunate people.
